About Hitseeker

Hitseeker is a project for the course 15-396, Science of the Web. The purpose of the assignment is to try to appear high up on Google when searching for the query hitseeker.

The class is taught at Carnegie Mellon University by Professor Luis Von Ahn. He's a pretty cool guy--check out his TED Talk.

The purpose of the course extends beyond hit seeking. In fact, the hitseeker assignment itself is merely an exercise to help seek understanding of page hit ranking. Other course assignments seek to hit concepts like social networks, online advertising, and privacy.
